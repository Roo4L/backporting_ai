# Backporting.ai

## Website Goals

* Assemble in one place all information related to patch backporting automation
    - Articles and research papers
    - Blog posts
    - Open source projects
    - Company releases and updates
* Update interested people on most important updates in the field (some email digest, for example)
* Provide people doing research in the field a platform for sharing their work
* Connect people doing research in the field and give the opportunity to collaborate, review, discuss

## Action plan / Implementation

* Create one page for collecting anything related to the patch backporting (in the way of references to the original sources)
* Create another for publishing digests
* Create an email subscription via which people may get the periodic digests
* Provide a way to submit new entries to the articles list (via email message, google form or something else)
* Extract authors from whatever resource we find on the topic and start to track per author metrics + provide details on how to contact the author
* Add a login page and provide people a way to leave comments under any artifact we reference on the site (alike to the Hacker News)
